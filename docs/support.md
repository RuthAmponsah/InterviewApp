## Interview Practice

### What types of interview questions does the app ask?

Our AI generates interview questions based on your job preferences, including:
- **Behavioral questions**: Tell me about a time when...
- **Technical questions**: Role-specific technical scenarios
- **Job-specific questions**: Questions tailored to your target role
- **General competency questions**: Communication, teamwork, problem-solving

### How long does an interview take?

Most interviews take **10-15 minutes**, depending on:
- Number of questions (typically 3-5)
- Interview mode (text or voice)
- Your response length

### Can I retake interviews?

✅ **Yes!** You can retake interviews anytime and track your progress over time. Compare your responses and feedback across multiple attempts.

### Can I practice voice interviews?

✅ **Yes!** Voice interviews are available in **Premium**. You can practice speaking your answers aloud, and our AI provides detailed feedback on your responses.

### What feedback do I receive after an interview?

You receive comprehensive feedback including:
- AI-powered response analysis
- Strengths and improvement areas
- Tips for better answers
- Comparison to your previous interviews

## Accounts & Authentication

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. On the **Sign In** screen, tap **Forgot Password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link (usually arrives within **5 minutes**)
4. Click the link and create a new password

### Can I use My Interview on multiple devices?

✅ **Yes!** Log in with the same email on any device and your interview history, progress, and settings will sync automatically.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy & Security**
2. Tap **Delete Account**
3. Follow the confirmation steps
4. Your data will be permanently deleted within **30 days**

### Can I change my email address?

Yes. Go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Change Email** and follow the instructions.

## Technical Issues

### The app keeps crashing. What should I do?

Try these steps in order:

1. **Force-close the app**
   - Swipe up from the bottom (iPhone) or use gesture to close
   - Wait 10 seconds
   - Reopen the app

2. **Restart your device**
   - Power off completely
   - Wait 30 seconds
   - Power on again

3. **Update the app**
   - Check the App Store for the latest version
   - Install any available updates

4. **Uninstall and reinstall**
   - Delete the app
   - Restart your device
   - Reinstall from the App Store

5. **Check your iOS version**
   - Settings → General → Software Update
   - Install the latest iOS

If the problem persists, [contact us](#contact-us).

### I'm having trouble with voice recognition. What can I do?

**Troubleshooting steps:**

1. **Check microphone permission**
   - Go to device Settings → **My Interview** → **Microphone**
   - Ensure "Allow" is selected

2. **Test your microphone**
   - Try recording a voice memo to ensure the mic works
   - Try a different app that uses voice

3. **Improve audio quality**
   - Use a quiet environment
   - Speak clearly and directly into the mic
   - Avoid background noise

4. **Restart the app**
   - Force-close My Interview
   - Reopen and try again

### The app won't load or shows a blank screen. What do I do?

**Step-by-step fix:**

1. **Check your internet connection**
   - Ensure you're on Wi-Fi or mobile data
   - Try another app to confirm connection works

2. **Disable VPN or proxy**
   - If you're using a VPN, temporarily disable it
   - Disable any ad-blocking apps

3. **Force-close and reopen**
   - Swipe up to close the app
   - Wait 10 seconds
   - Reopen the app

4. **Restart your device**
   - Power cycle your iPad or iPhone

5. **Reinstall the app**
   - Delete My Interview
   - Restart your device
   - Reinstall from the App Store

### How do I grant microphone permission?

1. Go to device **Settings**
2. Scroll down and tap **My Interview**
3. Tap **Microphone**
4. Select **Allow**

The app will also request permission the first time you use voice interviews.

### The app is slow or laggy. What can I do?

✅ Try these quick fixes:

- Close other apps running in the background
- Restart your device
- Clear the app cache: Settings → My Interview → Offload App, then reinstall
- Check available storage (My Interview needs at least 500 MB free)
- Update to the latest app version

## Data & Privacy

### Is my data safe?

✅ **Yes!** We prioritize your security with:

- **AES-256 encryption** for data at rest
- **TLS 1.3 encryption** for data in transit
- **Secure login** with encrypted passwords
- **Row-Level Security** ensuring you only access your own data

See our [Privacy Policy](/InterviewApp/privacy) for full details.

### Are my voice recordings stored?

❌ **No!** Voice recordings are:
- Processed **temporarily** on our AI service
- Used only for transcription
- **Automatically deleted** after processing
- **Never stored** on our servers

### Who has access to my interview data?

Only you! We use **Row-Level Security** policies to ensure:
- You can only view your own interviews
- No one else can access your data
- Admins cannot view user interviews

### How long do you keep my data?

- **Active accounts**: Data kept while your account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: Data permanently deleted within **30 days**
- **Backups**: Deleted data removed from backups within **90 days**

For full details, see our [Privacy Policy](/InterviewApp/privacy).
