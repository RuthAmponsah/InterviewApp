# Support - MY INTERVIEW App

## Getting Help

Need assistance with MY INTERVIEW? We're here to help!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get started with MY INTERVIEW?**  
A: Download the app, create an account, and complete your profile. You'll be ready to start practicing interviews in minutes!

**Q: Is the app free to use?**  
A: Yes! MY INTERVIEW offers a free tier with 2 interviews per month. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited interviews and advanced features like voice practice.

**Q: What devices does MY INTERVIEW support?**  
A: MY INTERVIEW works on iOS (iPad and iPhone), with Android support coming soon.

### Account & Authentication

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**  
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within minutes.

**Q: Can I use MY INTERVIEW on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes! Log in with the same email on any device and your interview history and settings will sync automatically.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Delete Account. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

### Interviews & Practice

**Q: What types of interview questions does MY INTERVIEW ask?**  
A: Our AI generates questions based on your job preferences, including behavioral questions, technical questions, and job-specific scenarios.

**Q: Can I practice voice interviews?**  
A: Yes! Voice interviews are available in Premium. You can practice speaking your answers aloud, and our AI will provide feedback.

**Q: How long does an interview take?**  
A: Most interviews take 10-15 minutes, depending on the mode and number of questions.

**Q: Can I retake interviews?**  
A: Yes! You can retake interviews and track your progress over time.

### Subscription & Payments

**Q: What's included in Premium?**  
A: Premium includes unlimited interviews, voice practice, advanced feedback, and access to sector-specific question packs.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Go to Settings > Subscription and tap "Manage Subscription" to cancel anytime.

**Q: Will I be charged when I cancel?**  
A: No. Cancellation is immediate and you won't be charged for the next billing period.

**Q: Can I use a promotional code?**  
A: Yes! Enter any valid promo code at checkout to get a discount.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app keeps crashing. What should I do?**  
A: Try:
1. Force-close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Uninstall and reinstall the app
4. Ensure you're running the latest version of MY INTERVIEW

**Q: I'm having trouble with voice recognition. What can I do?**  
A: Ensure:
1. Your microphone is enabled in Settings > Privacy & Security
2. You're in a quiet environment
3. You're speaking clearly
4. Your device's iOS is up to date

**Q: How do I grant mic permission?**  
A: Go to device Settings > MY INTERVIEW > Microphone > Allow

**Q: The app won't load. What do I do?**  
A: Try:
1. Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data)
2. Disable any VPN or proxy
3. Force-close and reopen the app
4. Restart your device

### Data & Privacy

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes. We use AES-256 encryption, secure login, and strict privacy controls. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy) for details.

**Q: Are my voice recordings stored?**  
A: No. Voice recordings are processed temporarily for transcription and then deleted. They're never stored on our servers.

**Q: Who has access to my interview data?**  
A: Only you. Our Row-Level Security policies ensure you can only access your own data.

**Q: How long do you keep my data?**  
A: We retain your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently delete all data within 30 days.
